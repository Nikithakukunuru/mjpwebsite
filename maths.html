DEPARTMENT OF TELUGU

About: The department has well qualified young and dynamic faculty members. We believe our students and their performance only can act as perfect testimonial. So our main objective is that to impart quality education to our students with best available facilities and devoted students sharing the enthusiasm for mathematics.


Includes: The bachelor of science in mathematics provides a strong background to the fundamentals of mathematics. There is a large demand for people with strong analytical skills and a broad-based background in the mathematical sciences. The three year Bsc programme will prepare a student to avail many diverse attractive opportunities.


    Objectives:
    On successful completion of their studies all students should
  • have developed problem solving skills.
  • have had the opportunity to take options outside their main area of study.
  • have covered the core topics of advanced mathematics which the department considers appropriate to their degree programme.
  • have learned to apply critical and analytical reasoning and to present logical and concise arguments.
  • be able to understand and present sophisticated mathematical arguments and rigorous proofs.
  • be able to comprehend high levels of abstraction in the study of pure mathematics.
  • be able to apply physical insight and mathematical techniques to the solution of problems in applied mathematics.
  • have had the opportunity to enhance their investigative, communication and presentation skills.
  • have to apply mathematical concepts and theorems to new situations.
    Programs offered:
  • Bsc MPC ( maths, physics, chemistry)
  • Bsc MPCs ( maths, physics, computer science)
  • Bsc MSCs ( maths, statistics, computer science)
  • Bsc MSDs ( maths, statistics, data science)
    Courses offered:
  • Differential and integral calculus
  • Differential equations
  • Real Analysis
  • Algebra
  • Linear algebra
  • Numerical analysis
  • Integral transforms
  • Analytical solid geometry
  • Theory of equations
  • Logic & sets
  • Number theory
  • Vector calculus
  • Basic mathematics
  • Mathematics for economics & finance
  • Mathematical modelling.
