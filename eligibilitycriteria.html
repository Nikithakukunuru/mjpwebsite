Eligibility Criteria

  • ELIGIBILITY TO APPEAR FOR RESIDENTIAL DEGREE COLLEGE COMMON ENTRANCE TEST (RDC-CET)
  • Students who passed Senior Intermediate Examination in March/April- of previous academic year conducted by the Board of Intermediate Education of Telangana or equivalent examination are ONLY eligible
  • The students should have passed the qualifying examination in one and first attempt in IPE-March and secure 50% of marks on aggregate and 40% of marks in English subject. (There is a relaxation of 5% in case of SC/ST). Students who appeared for Intermediate Examinations held in March are only eligible.
  • For B.A., (HEP & HPE) all the students are eligible irrespective of subjects studied in Intermediate. But they have to take Entrance Test in ENGLISH, HISTORY, ECONOMICS and CIVICS.
  • For B.Com., (General & Computers) students of CEC or any other group are eligible as per university norms. But they have to take the Entrance Test in ENGLISH, ECONOMICS & COMMERCE
  • For B.Sc., (MPC & MPCS) students of intermediate MPC, for B.Sc., (BZC) students of intermediate Bi.PC, are eligible to write examination.
  • For B.Sc., (MSCS) (Maths, Statistics & Computer Science) Inter MEC & MPC students are eligible to write entrance examination.
  • Annual Income of the Parents/Guardian shall not exceed Rs.1,50,000/- Rural areas and Rs.2,00,000/- in Urban Areas.
  • Jurisdiction of Candidates (RDC-CET)
  • The candidate, who has studied in any of the educational institutions, located in any district of Telangana State are eligible.
  • Private candidates should submit Nativity Certificate obtained from the MRO/Tahasildar. Residence Certificate will not be accepted.
  • Candidates must be the residents and must have studied in Telangana only.
  • d) The candidates must have appeared Intermediate or its equivalent exam in March/April in single attempt only are eligible. Supplementary & Instant candidates are not eligible for admission.
